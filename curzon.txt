
The Vicroy of India,Lord Curzon, had a vision- wanted to create a spectacular town hall for city.
In the year 1904, the foundation for this breathtking building was laid,but due to the partioningof Bengal,the Curzon Hall became Dhaka College in 1911. 
It took up its position as part of the science division of the University of Dhaka in 1921. 
The hall was also the scene of the student uprising against the language policy that the Mohammed Ali Jinnah tried to impose. Now there aremany departments of science faculty in curzon area.
