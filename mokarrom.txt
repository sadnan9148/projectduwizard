***************Mokarram Bhabhan Area*********************

Mokarram Bhaban area is situated just infront of Central Field of University of Dhaka.There is a huge building
named "Mokarram Bhaban". This buildig is named to show the honour to Mokarram Hossain Khundker who was a 
Bangladeshi scientist. He served as a professor at the chemistry department.

Departments :
CSE,Physics,Applied Statistics,Genetic Engineering , Pharmacy , IIT, Chemistry, EEE.
