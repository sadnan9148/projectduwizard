The Centre for Advanced Research in Sciences (CARS) was established in 2003 with the main objective of creating 
state-of-the-art research facilities for the faculty members, scientists and graduate students of different branches
 to carry out research, so that the standard of research in the University can be comparable to that of advanced laboratories of the World.
 The facilities of the Centre are being used by all the Departments of the faculties and Institutes of University of Dhaka.
